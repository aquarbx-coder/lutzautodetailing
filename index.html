About Lutz Auto Detailing

We're a locally owned and operated mobile detailing service based right here in Lutz, Florida. What started as a passion for cars grew into a mission: to give every vehicle the care it deserves — without the hassle of going to a shop.

We use professional-grade products, proven techniques, and a meticulous process for every job. Whether it's a daily commuter, a family SUV, or a weekend ride, we give the same level of attention to every vehicle.

Our Process

  1. Book Online — Pick your package, choose your time, and we handle the rest.
  2. We Show Up — We arrive at your location with everything we need.
  3. We Detail — Thorough, careful work from start to finish.
  4. You Enjoy — Walk out to a vehicle that looks and feels brand new.

Frequently Asked Questions

We're based in Lutz, FL and serve the surrounding areas including Land O' Lakes, Wesley Chapel, New Tampa, Odessa, and Carrollwood. If you're within about 20 miles of Lutz, we can likely come to you.

Access to a water spigot is helpful but not always required. We carry our own water supply and equipment for most services. We'll let you know during booking if we need anything specific.

A Basic Exterior takes about 1 hour. Interior Detail runs 1.5–2 hours. A Full Detail takes 3–4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. We never rush — quality comes first.

We use professional-grade, vehicle-safe products from trusted brands. All our cleaners are pH-balanced and safe for all paint types, leather, and interior surfaces.

We collect a small deposit at the time of booking to secure your appointment. The remaining balance is due upon completion. This helps us manage our schedule and ensures we can dedicate the time your vehicle deserves.

If severe weather is in the forecast, we'll contact you to reschedule at no extra cost. Light rain doesn't typically affect interior work, so we'll communicate with you on a case-by-case basis.
