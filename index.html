Common Questions

We're based in Lutz, FL and serve Land O' Lakes, Wesley Chapel, New Tampa, Odessa, and Carrollwood. If you're within about 20 miles, we can likely come to you.

Access to a water spigot is helpful but not always required. We carry our own water supply and equipment. We'll let you know during booking if we need anything.

A Basic Exterior takes about 1 hour. Interior Detail runs 1.5 to 2 hours. A Full Detail takes 3 to 4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

If severe weather is forecast, we'll contact you to reschedule at no extra cost. Light rain usually doesn't affect interior work.

We collect a small deposit when you book to secure your time slot. The remaining balance is due when the job is complete.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, and cash.
